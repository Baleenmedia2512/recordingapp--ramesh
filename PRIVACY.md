# Privacy Policy - Call Monitor

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Introduction

Call Monitor ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Call Log Information
- Phone numbers
- Call timestamps
- Call duration
- Call type (incoming, outgoing, missed)
- Contact names (if available)

### Call Recordings
- Audio recordings of phone calls (with your explicit permission)
- Recording metadata (duration, format, file size)

### Device Information
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifier
- Platform (Android/iOS)

### Account Information
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Full name (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Display your call history
   - Play recorded call audio
   - Sync data across your devices

2. **Improve Our Service**
   - Analyze app performance
   - Fix bugs and issues
   - Develop new features

3. **Ensure Security**
   - Authenticate your account
   - Protect against unauthorized access
   - Encrypt sensitive data

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Call recordings are stored locally on your device
- All recordings are encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
- Encryption keys are stored in Android Keystore/iOS Keychain

### Cloud Storage
- Metadata is stored on Supabase servers (secured with encryption)
- Optional: Audio files can be uploaded to secure cloud storage
- All data transfers use HTTPS encryption

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for recordings
- Row-level security on database
- Secure authentication with JWT tokens
- Regular security audits

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with third parties for marketing
- Disclose your call logs or recordings to anyone

We **MAY** share data:
- If required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights and safety
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: View all data we have about you
2. **Delete**: Remove your account and all associated data
3. **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
4. **Modify**: Update your account information
5. **Revoke**: Withdraw permissions at any time

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **Delete Account**: Go to Settings → Delete Account
- **Export Data**: Go to Settings → Export Data
- **Revoke Permissions**: Go to device Settings → Apps → Call Monitor → Permissions

## Platform-Specific Information

### Android
- Requires permissions: READ_CALL_LOG, READ_PHONE_STATE, RECORD_AUDIO, STORAGE
- Full call log and recording functionality
- Data stored in app-specific encrypted storage

### iOS
- Limited by Apple's privacy policies
- Cannot access system call logs
- Displays synced data from Android devices only
- VoIP call recording only (not regular calls)

## Children's Privacy

Call Monitor is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Data Retention

- Call logs: Retained until you delete them
- Recordings: Retained until you delete them
- Account data: Retained until you delete your account
- Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Changes to This Policy
